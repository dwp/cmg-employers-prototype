How to sign in to your employer account

If you deduct child maintenance from your employees’ earnings, you can manage your payments online.

You will need your employer reference number and PAYE number.

Sign in to your employer account to:

  • view the amounts you are due to deduct for specific employees
  • complete an online schedule that details your actual deductions and the total payment – we recommend this for smaller companies with up to 10 deduction from earnings orders (DEOs)
  • upload a report produced by your payroll software
  • view schedules and payments that you have made previously
  • send and receive secure messages between your company and us
  • keep a record of all communications between your company and us
  • tell us about a change in your company’s address and contact details
